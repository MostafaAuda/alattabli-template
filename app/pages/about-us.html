In the year 2020, we chose our name “Al-attabi construction company “ co. ltd reflecting our profession in the construction industry. our vision to be the pioneer in construction industry is leading us to play global role in construction of commercial & residential buildings, industrial and infrastructure projects.

AL-ATTABI Construction company defines its goals by putting all efforts, resources and dedication to A get international applicable standards In construction technology and at the same time provide of quality experience to the client .

We do follow engineering trends and latest technology of modern infrastructure with more efficient skills and optimum utilization in order to achieve goals that we share and prove with our valuable clients and to participate in the economic growth.

We have a strong diversified professional team of engineering personals with in depth knowledge of the industry and we deliver the projects to complete satisfaction of clients together with "safety first" as our primary goal  we are highly involved from dawn to dusk in activity and business of our projects.

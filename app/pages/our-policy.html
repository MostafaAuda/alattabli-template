Safety Policy

Start with what you have now. If you’re early in your career, the most important thing is to get your work out there and keep growing. Even if you’re not 100% confident the work is perfect or you don’t know exactly what to say about everything (especially the meaning of life!), get a portfolio online and start building a presence on creative community sites like Behance right away. You will refine your work and how you talk about it as you grow as a designer.

we are engaged in general contracting and construction business. We are strongly committed to provide a healthy and safe working environment for all employees, others and the society as a whole who may be affected by work activities at all the times. To enable us in achieving this AL –ATTABI has developed on OHSAS management system in line with OHSAS standards. In this regards , we are committed:

  • To prevent injuries and illnesses of employees due to work related activities.
  • To comply with all relevant applicable legal and other requirements including customer health and safety requirements.
  • To provide appropriate training to all employees to upgrade their understanding and meet individual OHSAS responsibilities and organizational needs.
  • To prevent injuries and illnesses of employees due to work related activities
